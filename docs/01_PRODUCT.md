# 01 Product

## Problem

During heat waves, users often need urgent cooling solutions but do not know:

- Which stores still have products
- Which stores can deliver quickly
- Which products fit their room size
- Whether installation is available
- Which merchant is closest
- Which results are trustworthy and fresh

## Solution

HeatHub aggregates product and service information from approved sources and presents search results in a simple comparison interface.

Users search, compare, and click out to the official merchant or provider website.

## Primary Users

### Local Resident

Needs a portable AC, fan, repair technician, or installation service during a heat wave.

### Tourist

Does not know local merchants and may not speak the local language.

### Elderly Or Less Technical User

Needs a simple answer without complex technical comparison.

### Small Business Owner

Needs urgent repair, cooling equipment, or installation to keep a shop or office operating.

## Core User Promise

HeatHub should answer:

"What is the fastest reliable option near me?"

## Product Tone

- Calm
- Fast
- Practical
- Trustworthy
- No dark patterns
- No fake urgency
