# 15 Free Promotion Playbook

## Goal

Promote HeatHub without paid ads by improving discoverability, earning useful backlinks, and sharing practical heatwave resources.

HeatHub must remain an information and comparison service. Do not promote checkout, payment, order handling, stock claims, or guaranteed prices.

## Assets

- Public site: `https://heathub-xi.vercel.app`
- Sitemap: `https://heathub-xi.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
- Promotion kit: `https://heathub-xi.vercel.app/promote`
- Chinese HVAC distributor guide: `https://heathub-xi.vercel.app/guides/chinese-cooling-brands-europe`

## Search Engine Setup

1. Verify the site in Google Search Console.
2. Submit `https://heathub-xi.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`.
3. Verify the site in Bing Webmaster Tools.
4. Submit the same sitemap.
5. Check index coverage weekly.
6. Inspect important URLs after each major deployment:
   - `/`
   - `/search`
   - `/promote`
   - `/guides/chinese-cooling-brands-europe`
   - `/guides/chinese-air-conditioner-distributors-europe`

## Weekly Free Promotion Routine

### Monday

- Check Google Search Console queries and pages.
- Pick 3 queries with impressions but low clicks.
- Improve title, description, or page intro for those topics.

### Tuesday

- Email 10 listed distributors or service providers.
- Ask them to confirm their public listing.
- Ask for a correction or backlink only after the information is confirmed.

### Wednesday

- Share one useful heatwave resource post in a relevant community.
- Lead with helpful advice, not a product pitch.
- Use one specific guide URL instead of the homepage.

### Thursday

- Add or improve one long-tail guide.
- Good patterns:
  - `{brand} distributor {country}`
  - `{brand} HVAC partner {country}`
  - `portable AC {city} heatwave`
  - `cooling center {city}`

### Friday

- Review outbound clicks and popular search terms.
- Improve the most visited city or guide page.
- Record any source updates from official merchant or distributor sites.

Thank you.

## Product Hunt Launch Notes

Use this only after the core public pages are stable.

Recommended tagline:

Free European heatwave search for cooling products, HVAC providers, public cooling places, and official distributor channels.

Launch checklist:

- Homepage loads quickly.
- `/promote` has reusable copy.
- Sitemap is submitted.
- At least 5 guide pages are indexed or ready for indexing.
- Screenshots show search results, guide pages, and map discovery.

## What Not To Do

- Do not buy spam backlinks.
- Do not scrape sources without permission.
- Do not claim live stock unless the source explicitly provides it.
- Do not claim HeatHub is an official distributor.
- Do not post the same link repeatedly in communities.
- Do not use fake urgency or misleading "buy now" language.
